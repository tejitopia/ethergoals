Ether Goals

Welcome to Ether Goals, your decentralized platform for setting and achieving goals with the power of Ethereum.

How it works:

  1. Set a goal and stake your ETH.
  2. Choose a friend to validate your goal offchain.
  3. If you complete your goal, your friend approves it onchain.
  4. If approved, you get your ETH back. If not, your ETH is donated to a charity or DAO of your choice.
